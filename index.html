About Us

ClaimSolver is an independent platform providing specialized claim file management and subrogation consulting services for the insurance sector.

We intervene in all branches of auto and non-auto claim files, especially in cases deemed uncollectible or unsolvable; we never abandon the process until it reaches resolution.

In addition to traffic-related files such as diminished value, claim differences, and fault modification requests, we provide effective solutions in complex subrogation processes involving CMR and FFL covered international transportation.

Specialists with 10+ years in international insurance claims, CMR/FFL subrogation, and cross-border recovery — managing every step from first notice to final collection.

Through communication, document review, and persistent follow-up in subrogation files, we make it possible to collect amounts that appear lost, managing not only the damage but the entire process.
